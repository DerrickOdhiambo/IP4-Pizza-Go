our story

Pizza & Go was first established in 2015 in Nairobi. We are a blend of quality pizza and an amazing team ready to attend to your pizza needs. We ensures you get only the best quality, fresh from he oven. We put a lot of effort iin ensuring that the sauce is made from fresh tomatoes to ensure our quality. Pizza is our heart and soul, and we want to share our taste experience with as many people as we can. Join us today and let's share great tastes and memories!
